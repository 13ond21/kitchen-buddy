3. Data that stays on your device

By default, pantry, timers, diary, pairings learned on device, waste log, bundles/kits, and settings are stored in local app storage (SQLite / app files). Android system backups of this app are disabled where the app configures allowBackup=false.

Features such as export backup (JSON), shopping list Markdown/PDF share, carer plain-text share, and QR pantry transfer only leave the device when you choose to share or show them. We do not operate a Kitchen Buddy server that receives your pantry automatically.

4. Optional network use

Those services have their own privacy policies.

Free users may have a daily limit on online lookups. Calm Deluxe / premium (including trial or care key) may remove that limit. Offline lookup against bundled or cached data does not require the network.

We do not sell your kitchen inventory or diary contents. We do not use the optional barcode lookup to build a marketing profile of your pantry.
